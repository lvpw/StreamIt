]> Overview of streamit.frontend.passes

Assorted passes on the StreamIt front-end IR. These passes generally are visitor classes on the front-end IR that replace parts of the IR tree with other parts. Thus, a class will typically extend {@link streamit.frontend.nodes.FEReplacer}; {@link streamit.frontend.passes.SymbolTableVisitor} is derived from this and maintains an active {@link streamit.frontend.nodes.SymbolTable} within each visitor function.
